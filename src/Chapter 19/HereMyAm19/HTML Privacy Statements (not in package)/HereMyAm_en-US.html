Here My Am! will attempt to access your location if you allow it, and will also use your webcam to take a picture, if you also allow it.

Pictures are automatically stored in your Pictures Library in a HereMyAm folder. Your location may be saved in HereMyAm's app data for the purpose of restarting the app if it's suspended and terminated.

Neither pictures nor your location are transmitted elsewhere unless you explicitly invoke the Share charm and choose to share these details with a target app.

No other personal information is collected, stored, or shared.

And don't quote me on how legally binding all of this is...it's just a sample!
